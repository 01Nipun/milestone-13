Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is a safe EMI level?
A: EMI should ideally be below 30% of income.

Q: How much should I save monthly?
A: At least 20% of income is recommended.

Q: What is DTI ratio?
A: Debt-to-Income ratio is EMI divided by income.

Q: Is taking a loan always bad?
A: No, but it depends on your financial health.

Q: What is a good financial profile?
A: High savings, low debt, and stable income.
