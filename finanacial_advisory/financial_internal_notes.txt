Internal Advisory Notes

Customers with high income sometimes overspend → hidden risk.
Some users under-report expenses → actual DTI may be higher.

Edge Cases:
- Freelancers → income variability
- Sudden large expenses → savings drop
- Users with zero EMI but zero savings → still risky

Important:
System recommendations are advisory only.
Final decisions depend on user behavior and external factors.

NOTE:
Not all users follow ideal financial discipline.
