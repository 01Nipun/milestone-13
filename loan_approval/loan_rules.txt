Loan Policy Rules:

1. Minimum Salary: 25,000 per month
2. Minimum Credit Score: 700
3. Maximum Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio: 55%
4. Interest Rate Range: 9% to 13%
5. Maximum Loan Amount: 20 times monthly salary
6. Maximum Tenure: 30 years
7. Existing EMI should not exceed 40% of salary
8. Stable job required for at least 1 year
9. Loan approval is higher for credit score above 750
